Bio

Samantha worked in the communications sector for 11 years before pivoting to web application development. Though she is a junior developer, she has the mind of a manager and a modern asthetic, which she uses to create useful and beautiful applications. Samantha uses tight project management and resourceful design to solve complex problems one step at a time. She brings a passion for supporting community and connection into each deliverable.

Samantha has worked in publishing, nonprofits, and the federal sector. She earned an MA in writing from The Johns Hopkins University and a BA in Communication and Media Studies from Goucher College. She will complete the Software Engineering Immersive at General Assembly in March 2021 and currently lives in Baltimore, MD.
